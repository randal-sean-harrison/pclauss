About

Patrick Clauss, Ph.D., is the director of Writing and Rhetoric in the University Writing Program. His research and teaching interests include rhetorical and argumentation theory, informal logic, and composition pedagogy.

He regularly teaches Multimedia Writing and Rhetoric and a graduate practicum on the teaching of writing. In the spring of 2014, he was named a recipient of the Rev. Edmund P. Joyce, C.S.C. Award for Excellence in Undergraduate Teaching.
