Samples

The student essays and exercises linked to here were written by previous WR students, all of whom have given me permission to share their work. In most cases, unless the student asked me not to, I have changed all names and all other identifying information. I have not changed identifying information in cases where sources are cited and the sources can be located online, in a library, etc. Note that these essays are not necessarily "perfect." Most of them are very good, but several are included to spur class discussion and reflection. (Some are models, quite frankly, of potentially good writing, but writing that needed serious revision.)

Remember that if you want to write about a topic already covered in one of these samples, you must discuss your ideas with me before beginning your own work.
